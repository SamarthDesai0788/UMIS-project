Welcome to UMIS Website

This website serves as the online headquarters of UMIS, providing valuable resources and information to a wide range of users, including current and prospective students, faculty, staff, and alumni. Through a focus on user-friendly design, accessibility, and comprehensive content, our aim is to foster engagement and collaboration within our college community.

Key Features:

  • Programs and Departments: Discover the many academic departments and programs we offer, each providing a unique opportunity for study and research in a variety of subjects.
  • Admissions: Find out more about our college's admissions procedure, prerequisites, and deadlines for potential students.
  • Student Resources: Get access to all of the necessary services and resources, such as campus facilities, student clubs, academic help, and more.
  • Faculty and Staff Directory: Establish a connection with our committed instructors and staff who support the college's active academic community.
  • Events and News: Keep yourself informed about forthcoming events, news, and announcements that pertain to the college community.
  • Alumni Network: Participate in interactions with other alumni, delve into success stories of alumni, and maintain connections with the college's extensive network.
  • Contact Us: Please do not hesitate to contact us for any inquiries, feedback, or assistance. We are available to provide you with support.

Regardless of whether you are currently enrolled as a student, part of the faculty, considering enrollment as a student, or an alum, we extend an invitation to peruse our website and uncover the array of opportunities and resources offered at UMIS.
